Swiss Alps Adventure

8 Days | 7 Nights | Guided Tour

Experience the Majesty of the Swiss Alps

Embark on an unforgettable journey through Switzerland's breathtaking Alpine landscapes. Our Swiss Alps Adventure takes you through picturesque villages, across stunning mountain passes, and to the most spectacular viewpoints in the region.

This meticulously planned 8-day tour combines outdoor activities, cultural experiences, and luxurious relaxation in some of Switzerland's finest accommodations.

Tour Highlights:

  • Cable car ride to Jungfraujoch - the "Top of Europe"
  • Guided hike through the Lauterbrunnen Valley
  • Cruise on Lake Geneva with views of Chillon Castle
  • Chocolate and cheese tasting in Gruyères
  • Scenic train ride on the Glacier Express route
  • Free time to explore charming villages like Zermatt and Montreux

Detailed Itinerary

Discover what each day of your Swiss adventure has in store

Day 1: Arrival in Zurich & Transfer to Interlaken

  • Morning arrival at Zurich Airport where you'll be met by your tour guide
  • Scenic train transfer to Interlaken (approx 2 hours)
  • Welcome lunch at a local restaurant featuring Swiss specialties
  • Afternoon orientation walk through Interlaken with your guide
  • Check-in to your luxury hotel with stunning mountain views
  • Welcome dinner at the hotel's restaurant

Day 2: Lauterbrunnen Valley & Jungfraujoch

  • Morning cable car ride to Jungfraujoch (3,454m elevation)
  • Explore the Ice Palace and observation decks with panoramic views
  • Lunch at Europe's highest-altitude restaurant
  • Afternoon hike through the Lauterbrunnen Valley ("Valley of 72 Waterfalls")
  • Visit the Trümmelbach Falls, a series of impressive glacial waterfalls inside the mountain
  • Evening at leisure in Interlaken

Day 3: Lucerne City & Lake Experience

  • Morning scenic train transfer from Interlaken to Lucerne
  • Guided tour of Lucerne’s Old Town, Chapel Bridge & Lion Monument
  • Lunch at a lakeside restaurant
  • Relaxing boat cruise on Lake Lucerne surrounded by the Alps
  • Evening at leisure for shopping or exploring Lucerne’s cafés
  • Overnight stay at a charming Lucerne hotel

Day 4: Mount Titlis Adventure

  • Morning excursion to Mount Titlis (3,020m)
  • Experience the world’s first rotating cable car – the Titlis Rotair
  • Walk across the thrilling Titlis Cliff Walk suspension bridge
  • Explore the Glacier Cave & enjoy snow activities
  • Lunch at a mountain-top restaurant with panoramic views
  • Return to Lucerne for an evening at leisure

Day 5: GoldenPass Journey to Zermatt

  • Scenic train ride on the famous GoldenPass route to Zermatt
  • Check-in to your hotel in the car-free alpine village
  • Afternoon visit to the Matterhorn Museum to learn about mountaineering history
  • Evening leisure walk through Zermatt’s cozy streets
  • Dinner featuring traditional Swiss raclette or fondue

Day 6: Gornergrat & Matterhorn Views

  • Morning ride on the Gornergrat cogwheel railway
  • Enjoy breathtaking views of the Matterhorn and surrounding peaks
  • Lunch at a panoramic restaurant overlooking glaciers
  • Afternoon alpine hike or optional helicopter tour
  • Evening free for shopping or spa relaxation in Zermatt

Day 7: Return to Zurich & City Highlights

  • Morning train journey back to Zurich
  • Guided tour of Zurich – Bahnhofstrasse, Old Town & Lindenhof Hill
  • Lunch at a gourmet restaurant
  • Afternoon boat ride on Lake Zurich
  • Evening farewell dinner with your group
  • Overnight stay in Zurich

Day 8: Departure from Zurich

  • Breakfast at the hotel
  • Free time for last-minute shopping or relaxing at a café
  • Private transfer to Zurich Airport for your flight home
  • End of your unforgettable Swiss adventure
