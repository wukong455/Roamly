Japanese Cultural Journey

10 Days | 9 Nights | Guided Tour

Discover the Essence of Japan

Immerse yourself in Japan's rich cultural heritage with our carefully curated journey through historic temples, traditional arts, and modern wonders. From the ancient capital of Kyoto to the neon-lit streets of Tokyo, this tour offers an authentic exploration of Japan's timeless beauty.

Experience tea ceremonies with masters, stay in ryokan inns, witness geisha performances, and learn age-old crafts - all while traveling comfortably between destinations on Japan's famed bullet trains.

Tour Highlights:

  • Private tea ceremony with a tea master in Kyoto
  • Traditional kaiseki dining experience
  • Stay in authentic ryokan with onsen hot springs
  • Cultural workshops (calligraphy, origami, kimono dressing)
  • Visits to UNESCO World Heritage Sites
  • Shinkansen (bullet train) travel between cities
  • Evening with maiko (apprentice geisha) performance

Detailed Itinerary

Your journey through Japanese culture

Day 1-2: Arrival in Tokyo & Cultural Introduction

  • Arrival at Narita/Haneda Airport with private transfer
  • Orientation walking tour of Asakusa district
  • Visit Senso-ji Temple (Tokyo's oldest)
  • Traditional wagashi (Japanese sweets) making workshop
  • Evening izakaya (Japanese pub) dining experience
  • Morning sumo stable visit (watching practice session)
  • Afternoon tea ceremony workshop

Day 3-4: Hakone & Mt. Fuji

  • Shinkansen ride to Odawara Station
  • Cable car ride to Owakudani volcanic valley
  • Cruise on Lake Ashi with views of Mt. Fuji
  • Check-in to traditional ryokan with private onsen
  • Kaiseki dinner with seasonal ingredients
  • Morning visit to Hakone Open-Air Museum
  • Traditional washi paper making workshop
  • Evening return to Tokyo

Day 5-6: Cultural Kyoto

  • Morning Shinkansen to Kyoto
  • Visit Fushimi Inari's famous torii gate tunnels
  • Private tea ceremony with master
  • Explore Gion district (geisha area)
  • Kimono dressing and photoshoot experience
  • Morning Zen meditation session
  • Visit Kinkaku-ji (Golden Pavilion) and Ryoan-ji rock garden
  • Evening maiko (apprentice geisha) performance

Day 7-8: Nara & Osaka

  • Day trip to Nara - visit Todai-ji Temple with giant Buddha
  • Walk through Nara Park with sacred deer
  • Traditional calligraphy workshop
  • Evening in Osaka's Dotonbori district
  • Osaka Castle visit
  • Kuromon Ichiba market food tour
  • Origami workshop with master

Day 9-10: Return to Tokyo & Departure

  • Final morning in Kyoto (free time)
  • Shinkansen return to Tokyo
  • Visit Meiji Shrine and Harajuku district
  • Farewell dinner with sake tasting
  • Morning visit to Tsukiji Outer Market
  • Private transfer to airport
