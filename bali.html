Bali Tropical Escape

7 Days | 6 Nights | Guided Tour

Discover Paradise in Bali

Immerse yourself in the enchanting beauty of Bali with our 7-day luxury tropical escape. From lush jungles to pristine beaches, ancient temples to vibrant culture - this carefully curated tour showcases the very best of the Island of Gods.

Experience perfect harmony of relaxation and adventure as we take you through sacred temples, emerald rice terraces, and crystal-clear waters teeming with marine life - all while enjoying world-class Balinese hospitality.

Tour Highlights:

  • Private sunrise trek up Mount Batur with breakfast cooked by volcanic steam
  • Luxury beachfront accommodation in Seminyak and Ubud
  • Traditional Balinese cooking class with a local family
  • Snorkeling adventure at pristine Nusa Penida island
  • Sacred water purification ceremony at Tirta Empul Temple
  • Private sunset cruise with seafood dinner
  • Spa day featuring authentic Balinese massage

Detailed Itinerary

Your perfect week in paradise

Day 1: Arrival in Bali - Seminyak Beachfront

  • Arrival at Ngurah Rai International Airport with VIP fast-track service
  • Private transfer to your luxury beachfront villa in Seminyak
  • Welcome drink and traditional Balinese blessing ceremony
  • Leisurely evening to relax by your private pool
  • Welcome dinner at a renowned beach club with sunset views

Day 2: Cultural Ubud & Sacred Monkey Forest

  • Morning transfer to Ubud with stops at Celuk silver village and Batuan Temple
  • Interactive Balinese cooking class with a local family
  • Guided walk through the sacred Monkey Forest sanctuary
  • Visit to the iconic Tegallalang Rice Terraces
  • Check-in to your luxurious jungle retreat overlooking the Ayung River
  • Evening traditional Kecak fire dance performance

Day 3: Sunrise Trek & Hot Springs

  • Early morning trek up Mount Batur for breathtaking sunrise views
  • Breakfast cooked by volcanic steam at the summit
  • Relaxation at Toya Devasya natural hot springs
  • Afternoon return to your hotel for leisure time
  • Optional spa treatments or river rafting adventure

Day 4: Water Temples & Spiritual Cleansing

  • Morning visit to Tirta Empul Temple for sacred water purification
  • Explore the royal water temple of Pura Taman Saraswati
  • Afternoon visit to a local coffee plantation for luwak coffee tasting
  • Sunset drinks at the picturesque Campuhan Ridge Walk

Day 5: Nusa Penida Island Adventure

  • Speedboat transfer to Nusa Penida Island
  • Snorkeling at Manta Point and Crystal Bay
  • Visit to Kelingking Beach's famous T-Rex cliff viewpoint
  • Lunch with ocean views at Angel's Billabong
  • Return to mainland Bali in the late afternoon

Day 6: Luxury Beach Day & Sunset Cruise

  • Morning at leisure to enjoy your villa's amenities
  • Private beach cabana at Potato Head Beach Club
  • Afternoon Balinese massage at a luxury spa
  • Charter sunset cruise with cocktails and seafood dinner
  • Farewell fireworks display

Day 7: Departure

  • Morning yoga session overlooking the ocean
  • Free time for last-minute shopping in Seminyak
  • Private transfer to the airport with VIP Fast Track service
